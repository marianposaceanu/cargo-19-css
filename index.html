01 / Foundation

Design principles

Signal first

Color and geometry expose state quickly; labels preserve meaning when color is unavailable.

Built, not polished

Panels feel manufactured and maintainable rather than ornamental or cinematic.

Modern underneath

Cascade layers, custom properties, container units, native dialog, reduced motion, and keyboard tabs.
